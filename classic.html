About

I am a fourth-year physics undergraduate at the Middle East Technical University (METU / ODTÜ) in Ankara, focusing on quantum optics and quantum sensing. I am interested in how the quantum behaviour of light and matter can be used to measure the physical world with precision beyond classical limits.

I am a TÜBİTAK scholar and intend to pursue graduate study in experimental quantum science.

Research Interests

  • Quantum optics — light–matter interaction and the statistics of light
  • Quantum sensing — measurement beyond the standard quantum limit
  • Photonics — interferometry, optical cavities, and quantum light sources
  • Quantum metrology and parameter estimation
