About

Who I am and what drives my curiosity.

I am a 4th-year physics undergraduate at the Middle East Technical University (METU / ODTÜ) in Ankara, where my work centers on quantum optics and quantum sensing. I am fascinated by how the fundamental behaviour of light and matter can be harnessed to measure the physical world with precision beyond classical limits.

As a TÜBİTAK scholar, I divide my time between coursework in advanced physics and hands-on research in optical and quantum measurement. My long-term goal is to pursue graduate study in experimental quantum science, building instruments and experiments that turn quantum phenomena into practical sensing technologies.
